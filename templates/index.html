About Mumbai

Mumbai, often called the "City of Dreams," is India's financial capital and a vibrant metropolis on the western coast. Known for its bustling streets, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive, it blends tradition with modernity. As a hub for Bollywood, commerce, and innovation, Mumbai attracts millions with its fast-paced lifestyle and endless opportunities, all set against the backdrop of the Arabian Sea.
